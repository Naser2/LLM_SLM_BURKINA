## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Current State Analysis

The Alliance of Sahel States (AES) - Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger - face **severe development challenges** but possess **untapped AI opportunities**. This report identifies 159 African AI companies with $803M in funding, analyzes 50+ startups across agriculture, healthcare, education, and language technology, and proposes a **$10-15M, 5-year strategic roadmap** to establish AES as a **Francophone AI innovation hub**.

### Critical Findings

#### 1. **The Funding Gap**
- **Francophone Africa receives <20% of VC funding** despite 40% of African population
- **Burkina Faso, Mali, Niger:** Virtually zero AI startups currently
- **Opportunity:** First-mover advantage in Francophone Sahel AI market

#### 2. **The Infrastructure Crisis**
- **Electricity Access:** 30% in Burkina Faso, 25% in Mali, 20% in Niger
- **Internet Penetration:** <10% rural areas
- **Doctor Shortage:** 1:10,000+ population ratio
- **Teacher Shortage:** 1:80-100 students in rural areas

#### 3. **The Language Emergency**
- **Moore (7M speakers):** ZERO NLP resources
- **Fulfulde (25M speakers):** Virtually no AI support
- **Bambara (14M speakers):** Google Translate only (low quality)
- **Educational Impact:** Students taught in French (not mother tongue), 30-50% comprehension gap

#### 4. **The Sectoral Urgency**
- **Agriculture:** 80% of workforce, yields declining 10-15% per decade (drought)
- **Healthcare:** 30-50% counterfeit drugs, 2M+ displaced persons need services
- **Education:** 40-60% enrollment, 2-4 grade levels behind

### Strategic Recommendation: **The AES AI Leap**

Rather than replicate West African hubs (Lagos, Accra, Dakar), **leverage the AES alliance** to build a **Francophone Sahel-specific AI ecosystem** addressing unique regional challenges (drought, displacement, linguistic isolation, energy scarcity).
